Akim Kotelnikov

I am an ML researcher at Yandex.Research, mostly working on deep learning models for tabular data. Previously, I had internships as a Quant Researcher at Pinely and as an ML Engineer at Yandex. I obtained my BSc and MSc degrees at HSE University.

I am mostly intereseted in deep learning for tabular data with main focus on pushing limits of neural networks -- for example, developing new state-of-the-art architectures or approaches, exploring tabular foundational models, applying diffusion models for tabular data. I am also interested in deep learning applications in trading and portfolio optimization.
